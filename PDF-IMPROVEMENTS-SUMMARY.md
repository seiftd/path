### 4. **جدول BMC ممتلئ بالبيانات** 📊

#### قبل:
- جدول BMC **فارغ** ❌

#### بعد:
- جدول BMC **ممتلئ بإجابات المستخدم** ✅
- تنسيق احترافي مع Word Wrap
- عرض واضح لجميع الأقسام التسعة:
  1. Value Propositions
  2. Customer Segments
  3. Customer Relationships
  4. Channels
  5. Key Partners
  6. Key Activities
  7. Key Resources
  8. Cost Structure
  9. Revenue Streams

```

```

```
[تحليل مخصص حسب:

 - المجال]
```

### 6. **أمثلة على التحليل المخصص**:

#### مثال 1: Value Propositions - مشروع زراعي في مصر
```
💡 Strategic Insights:
For agricultural projects in Egypt, focus on: sustainable 
practices, local food security, organic certification 
opportunities, and export potential. Consider partnerships 
with agricultural cooperatives and government agricultural 
programs.
```

#### مثال 2: Key Partners - مشروع في السعودية
```
💡 Strategic Insights:
In Saudi Arabia, explore: Vision 2030 initiatives, Monsha'at 
SME Authority support, partnerships with accelerators (Badir, 
KAUST), and local manufacturing incentives.
```

#### مثال 3: Revenue Streams - مشروع SaaS
```
💡 Strategic Insights:
SaaS revenue models: monthly/annual subscriptions, tiered 
pricing, usage-based fees, enterprise licenses. Consider 
local payment methods (cash on delivery, Fawry, mada) and 
regional pricing strategies.
```
